Built-In Assessment and Progress Monitoring

A simple, fast, and predictive system to identify risk, guide instruction, and measure growth.

Literacy Readiness Assessment

Fast, Accurate Screening

The IntraAct literacy readiness assessment quickly identifies where each student stands and what they need to progress.

2–5 Minutes Per Student

Quick administration means teachers can screen an entire class efficiently without disrupting instruction time.

Identifies Early Literacy Skills

Measures foundational skills including letter knowledge, phonemic awareness, and decoding ability to pinpoint each student's level.

Detects Risk Factors Immediately

Early identification of at-risk students allows teachers to intervene before gaps widen, preventing long-term reading difficulties.

Step-Based System

Clear Progression Through Mastery Levels

Students move step-by-step through defined levels, advancing only after demonstrating mastery at each stage.

1

Pre-Reader

Students build foundational skills including letter recognition, phonemic awareness, and early sound-letter connections.

2

Early Reader

Students begin decoding simple words and sentences, applying phonics knowledge to connected text with guided support.

3

Reader

Students read with increasing fluency and accuracy, building automaticity and beginning to comprehend more complex texts.

4

Literate

Students demonstrate functional literacy with strong fluency, vocabulary, and comprehension across a range of texts.

Data Tracking

Continuous Digital Progress Monitoring

Every assessment result is digitally tracked, giving teachers and administrators a clear, real-time picture of student growth and instructional effectiveness.

  • All results digitally tracked and stored
  • Progress continuously monitored across mastery levels
  • Instruction adapts based on individual student performance
  • Teachers receive actionable data to guide daily instruction
  • Administrators can monitor cohort-level trends and outcomes
Predictive Validity

Early Skills Predict Long-Term Reading Success

Students who reach higher levels within the IntraAct system demonstrate strong performance on standard assessments. The skills measured early in the process are reliable predictors of long-term reading outcomes.

Strong Assessment Performance

Students who reach higher IntraAct levels consistently demonstrate strong performance on standardized reading assessments.

Predictive Early Indicators

Early literacy skills measured by IntraAct are reliable predictors of long-term reading success and academic achievement.

DIBELS Alignment

Aligned with Established Frameworks

IntraAct assessments align with the DIBELS framework, ensuring compatibility with widely-used literacy benchmarks.

Letter Naming Fluency

Measures how quickly and accurately students can identify letter names, a foundational skill for early reading development.

Phoneme Segmentation Fluency

Assesses the ability to segment spoken words into individual phonemes, a critical component of phonemic awareness.

Oral Reading Fluency

Measures reading speed, accuracy, and expression on grade-level passages, a key indicator of overall reading proficiency.

Reading Comprehension

Evaluates students' ability to understand and retell what they read, ensuring decoding skills translate into meaningful literacy.
