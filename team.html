Method Developers

The Founders of IntraAct

The IntraAct method was developed by leading experts in cognitive neuroscience and reading development.

Fritz Jansen

Co-Developer, IntraAct Method

Psychologist and researcher specializing in cognitive neuroscience and learning processes. Co-developed the IntraAct literacy method based on research into how the brain acquires reading skills.

Uta Streit

Co-Developer, IntraAct Method

Psychologist and educator with deep expertise in reading development and cognitive science. Co-developed the IntraAct method to translate neuroscience research into effective classroom instruction.

Organization

IntInt: International Literacy Leadership

The IntraAct method is internationally led by IntInt, an organization of committed educators, literacy specialists, and implementation experts working to improve reading outcomes worldwide.

Our team includes professionals with expertise in:

  • Reading science and structured literacy
  • Teacher training and professional development
  • School and district implementation
  • Educational research and assessment
  • International education programs
