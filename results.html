How IntraAct Compares

Faster Outcomes Than Traditional Approaches

IntraAct delivers results in a fraction of the time compared to conventional literacy programs.

Traditional Approaches

Can take up to 2 years to achieve similar literacy outcomes. Progress is often slow, inconsistent, and difficult to measure, leaving many students behind.

The IntraAct Method

The majority of students reach functional literacy within 1 school year. Structured, systematic instruction accelerates progress and produces measurable gains from the first weeks.

Student Progression

A Clear Path to Literacy

Students follow a structured progression from foundational skills to fluent, independent reading.

1

Month 1

Letter recognition and blending. Students learn to identify letters and begin combining sounds into simple words.

2

Month 4

Confident decoding and simple reading. Students decode words accurately and read simple sentences with growing confidence.

3

Month 9

Fluent reading with comprehension. Students read connected text fluently and demonstrate understanding of what they read.
