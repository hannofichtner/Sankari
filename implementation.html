Implementation for Schools

A structured process that helps schools and districts adopt the IntraAct literacy method effectively.

Implementation Model

Four Steps to Effective Literacy Instruction

Our structured implementation model ensures schools are supported at every stage of adoption.

1

Teacher Training

Educators participate in professional development sessions introducing the instructional framework and core methodology.

2

Classroom Integration

Teachers incorporate structured reading activities into their existing literacy instruction with guided support.

3

Coaching & Support

Educators receive ongoing guidance and coaching during the implementation process to ensure fidelity.

4

Progress Monitoring

Student reading progress is tracked through assessments and literacy benchmarks to measure impact.

Professional Development

Comprehensive Teacher Training

Our professional development program equips educators with the knowledge and skills to deliver effective literacy instruction using the IntraAct method.

  • Understanding the science of reading and cognitive foundations
  • Mastering the IntraAct instructional framework
  • Practicing guided reading and feedback techniques
  • Learning assessment and progress monitoring tools
  • Building confidence in structured literacy delivery
Ongoing Support

We Support You at Every Stage

Implementation success depends on sustained support. We partner with schools throughout the entire process.

Coaching

Dedicated coaching support helps teachers refine their practice and troubleshoot challenges during implementation.

Materials

Comprehensive instructional manuals and classroom resources are provided to support consistent, high-quality instruction.

Assessment

Progress monitoring tools and literacy benchmarks help schools track student growth and measure program effectiveness.
