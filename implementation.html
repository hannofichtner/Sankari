Training

Practical, Focused Teacher Training

IntraAct training is designed for real classrooms. Teachers and coordinators are ready to implement immediately after completing their sessions.

  • Coordinator training: 1.5–2 hours
  • Teacher training: 2–3 hours total
  • Focus on practical application and classroom use

Ongoing Coaching and Guidance

Teachers are never on their own. IntraAct provides continuous support throughout implementation.

Live Coaching via Zoom

Ongoing coaching sessions via Zoom keep teachers supported and confident as they implement the method.

Continuous Guidance
