Implementation Model

Four Steps to Fast, Practical Literacy Implementation

A simple, ready-to-deploy model that trains teachers in under two hours and delivers rapid results for both early learners and students who have fallen behind.

1

Teacher Training

Teachers and coordinators are trained in under two hours to deliver the method, whether teaching students to read from scratch or accelerating those who are behind.

2

Classroom Integration

Students engage in structured, 40-minute daily sessions that build decoding automaticity and reading fluency within existing classroom routines.

3

Coaching & Support

Ongoing coaching, rapid feedback, and simple guidance ensure high-quality instruction with minimal burden on teachers.

4

Progress Monitoring

Quick, simple assessments track mastery and progress, showing measurable gains and rapid movement toward functional literacy.

Professional Development

Comprehensive Teacher Training

Our streamlined training equips teachers and coordinators in under two hours to confidently deliver the IntraAct method with immediate classroom impact.

  • Understanding the science of reading, cognitive foundations, and automaticity
  • Mastering the IntraAct instructional framework and structured daily routines
  • Practicing guided reading, rapid-response teaching, and feedback techniques
  • Learning simple assessments and progress monitoring tools
  • Building confidence with ongoing coaching, support, and real-time feedback
Ongoing Support

We Support You at Every Stage

Implementation success depends on sustained, simple support. We partner with schools throughout the entire process.

Coaching

Ongoing coaching with rapid feedback (often within 24 hours) via Zoom/WhatsApp helps teachers refine instruction and ensure fidelity.

Materials

Students receive structured IntraAct books (reading, writing, activities) and teachers receive clear manuals—simple, ready-to-use materials.

Assessment

Quick literacy readiness assessments (2–5 minutes) and continuous progress tracking provide clear data on mastery and growth.

Who It's For

Designed for Every Learner

IntraAct was originally developed for neurodivergent learners—and has shown even stronger results with the general student population, making it a universal method for learning to read.

Neurodivergent Learners

Proven effective for students with dyslexia, autism, ADHD, and other learning differences through structured, explicit instruction that builds decoding automaticity.

All Learners

Equally effective for beginners learning to read from the start and for students who have fallen behind—rapidly accelerating them to functional literacy.

Schools, Systems & Organizations

Adopt at any scale with simple training, ready-to-use materials, and built-in assessments for fast, measurable impact.
