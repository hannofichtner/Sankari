What IntraAct Is

A Structured, Neuroscience-Based Literacy Method

IntraAct is a structured, neuroscience-based literacy method designed to build strong decoding, fluency, and comprehension skills through explicit, systematic instruction.

Aligned with the Science of Reading, IntraAct ensures that every student develops the foundational skills required for lifelong literacy.

Designed for Every Learner

Built for the Most Challenging Learners. Effective for All.

IntraAct was originally developed for students with dyslexia, autism, and other learning differences.

Because of this, it is exceptionally effective for all learners.

Students who struggle most benefit first. All students benefit more.
